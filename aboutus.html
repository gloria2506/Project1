WHO ARE WE

Waynes Modelling Agency started 2015 and since then has expanded tremediously. it has become a multidimentional modelling agency, having brilliance in the art of beauty,style and creativity in our models that meet all standards.

Wayne has proven to be one of the best in Nigeria and Africa, providing quality services for commercial,editorial and print.

We groom Nigeria's best models and taking the industry to finer heights. The agency has created a platform that sees young aspiring models from all over. Training is done and the strengths and skills of each and every one is incresed to finest points.

Wayne agency is mother to some of Nigeria and Africa's top models; Beyonce,Ibiam Claire Grande, Nwaokorie Oluchi,Ologunro Wura, Zoe Mairo and others
